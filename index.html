About the foundation

Who Was Esther Idisi?

Esther Idisi dedicated her life to practical compassion. She advocated for abused women, supported orphanages, prepared meals, and cared for the less privileged with dignity and tenderness.

The foundation established in her honour continues that same work through community support and a yearly basic medical outreach for families who need care most.

She did not wait to be called. She simply showed up.

Free medical outreach

Free Basic Medical Outreach

For now, the foundation hosts an annual basic medical outreach where community members can receive simple health checks, wellness support, vitamins, supplements, and eye care at no cost.

  • Supplements and vitamins
  • Free medicated glasses
  • Eye checkups and basic vision support
  • Blood pressure checks
  • Care offered with dignity and warmth
